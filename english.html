Nr.1 Youth Culture Radiostation in Transylvania!


Lauschbox is a one of a kind radio project, founded in 2008, in Romania. It is the only multilingual youth radio from Transylvania.

Our shows are broadcasted in many languages, such as German, English, French, Romanian, Dutch and Hungarian.

The shows are created with a youthful audience in mind, giving valuable information to the students and other linguistic minorities of Transylvania. We want to deliver a professional message through quality journalism for better understanding of today’s youth.


What do we do?

We provide an insight on culture from the heart of Transylvania, in Cluj-Napoca.

Only the best music, from classics to brand-new hit songs of tomorrow. We want to stay away from the mainstream media of other chart-radios. It does not matter whether you are an alternative, indie, electronica or underground music fan, Lauschbox is a place for you and your friends!

Also, we are constantly scouting for fellow colleagues, so if you want to practice your journalism skills, Lauschbox offers long or short term internships.

Our broadcasts are of high quality and provide a meaningful insight on what matters for today’s youth.

We promote the foreign deparments of the Babeș-Bolyai University of Cluj-Napoca, especially (but not limited to) the German department.


What we plan to do:

We want to promote the great cultural diversity of Transylvania.

To some of today’s youth, the connection between how society works and politics is shallow; this is why we want to make them more accessible to our listeners.

We want to make journalism interesting for the younger generation.

The number of foreign students in Transylvania is growing fast each year. Lauschbox wants to give these international “urban dwellers” a second hometown, thei own “home away from home”.
